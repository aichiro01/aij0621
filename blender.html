Blender Environment

This was a three-dimensional construction done within Blender. Here are a mixture of raw geometric shapes and modified geometric shapes that form a landscape filling out the entire camera frame. The objects seen portray a underground cave village with buildings/structures and the element of light gives off the feeling by highlighting the inner workings of the cave, and having the dark background having a major presence.



Blender Donut

This three-dimensional render of a donut was created within a software called Blender. Here there are elements of geometric modification, lighting, subsurface coloring, textures, and manipulation. By adding a natural light source element there is a better sense of weight and feel of it being actually present.
