About Me

Bio

Over the years of being an artist I have worked in many different mediums, using several techniques/materials constructing my art. However, out of all the sculpting and charcoal drawings I have done nothing will ever beat the feeling of a sketchbook, my hands, a pencil, and my brain to let my mind drift and doodle. That to me is my identity. Someone who crafts based upon creativity and loose thoughts.
The concept of drawing on pen and paper allowed e to discover digital 2-d art, and for over a year now I have taken my art from paper to within a computer and wish to allow my mind to be free not only within the reality, but within the digital world. Now that you know and understand where I stand as an artist allow me to take you through my works, and show/explain what they are.
